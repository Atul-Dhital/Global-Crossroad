Join Us on an Unforgettable Journey of Hope

At Global Crossroad, Adventure Charity Travel is something we've always loved. There’s something incomparable to the feeling of exploring a foreign land, culture, or nature, while also helping to make this world a better place. We are a US based Charity travel company organizing life-changing adventure charity holidays in some of the best destinations of Asia, Latin America and Africa since 2003.

We take great pride in our customer satisfaction, with 98% of our clients recommending us to their friends. You can read testimonials on Go Overseas and Go Abroad. Our main objective is to help make this world a better place. For this cause, we donate 50% of your money to the Asna Child Rehabilitation Home, a home that supports and raises the vulnerable children.

We make every effort to make your trip enriching while also enhancing the communities we travel to. We collaborate with fully licensed, highly experienced & knowledgeable local guides, restaurants, and guesthouses. Join us on your dream charity vacation at an extremely affordable rates with no hidden fees.
